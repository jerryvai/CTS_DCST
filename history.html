THE CALCUTTA TECHNICAL SCHOOL

The Calcutta Technical School was developed in 1926. It was one of the leading engineering college at Kolkata. Initially it was a school, after 10th examination people join here to upgrade and shape their knowledge. British raj developed this institution to educate illiterate british peoples for new born companies. Besides some Indian neriturious students can also join this institution. Initially it was started with two branches of engineeering; i.e. Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering. This is a diploma college so as it is renowened for Mechanical & Electrical Engineering this students got 100% placement in varrious MNCs. besides in this college there are two engineering branches are more included after decade. These are Civil Engineering & Computer science & Technology. Teachers are very kind & generoud. They teach everyone well. The students of this colleges are well stablished in varrious MNCs.This college is one the heritage site of the heritagw city Kolkata.This college building is build by red coloured rock same as the structure of 'lal Kothi' of British Raj. oue renowned Principle is Dr. Debobrata Pradhan.
